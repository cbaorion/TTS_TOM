A Resource for studies of Low-Mass Pre-Main Sequence stars in Orion

This project aims at bringing together the extensive information collected by the CIDA Variability Survey study of the Orion OB1 Association and related publications on the young stellar population of this nearby star forming region. Here we consolidate all existing observations for every confirmed PMS members of the Orion OB1 association.
